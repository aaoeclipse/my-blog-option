# The Covid Vaccine

The journey of the Covid vaccine has been a rollercoaster ride of contention and misinformation, despite the persistent efforts of the scientific community to maintain clarity and transparency. Research papers have consistently provided reliable information, yet the interplay of media coverage and political debate has, unfortunately, overshadowed the significant achievement of developing this groundbreaking vaccine. In this blog I'll try to debunk many of the missinformation surrounding the Covid vaccination while defending the position that getting the vaccine was the best decision to make. But first, I want to make clear what I mean by "best decision to make". 

![decision](/imgs/vaccines/decision.gif)

When making a decision in the real world, normally it's not a theoretically perfect bad and good options. It's messy and there are risk between both of the options.
